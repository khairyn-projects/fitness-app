A fitness app designed to help students and young adults monitor their daily physical activity, exercise routines, and wellness goals.  The app encourages healthier lifestyles by providing progress tracking, goal setting, and activity monitoring in a simple and friendly user-interface.

## Project Description 
A fitness app designed to help students and young adults monitor their daily physical activity, exercise routines, and wellness goals. 
The app encourages healthier lifestyles by providing progress tracking, goal setting, and activity monitoring in a simple and friendly user-interface.
