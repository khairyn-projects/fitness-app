# Results Summary
- The testing results show that the GetFit prototype performs well overall and is easy for users to navigate. 
- Testers were able to complete key tasks such as recording workouts, viewing progress, and setting goals without difficulty.
- Most testers found the system intuitive, clear, and helpful for tracking their fitness activities, indicating that the user flow and interface design are effective.
- The core features, including GPS workout tracking, manual exercise logging, goal setting, and progress monitoring, were generally well received and functioned as intended in the prototype.
- While the feedback was largely positive, testers suggested minor improvements such as enhancing visual elements and adding more motivational features to further improve the overall user experience.
- Overall, the testing results indicate that the prototype provides a strong foundation for a functional fitness tracking application.
