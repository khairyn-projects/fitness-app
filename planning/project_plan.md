# Agile Process / Trello Usage 
- Trello was the main tool used to track our project’s progress.
- To keep things neat, we utilised two Trello boards to track the project’s progression.

- The first Trello board was a general timeline of the entire SDLC project.
- It detailed tasks and sorted them by their weekly Sprints. Due to the fact that the team is following the Agile methodology, this task list was not designed to be static.

- On the other hand, the second Trello board was more of our bread and butter.
- The second board takes a closer look at each Sprint, sorting tasks into three categories: “To-do”, “Doing” and “Done”.
